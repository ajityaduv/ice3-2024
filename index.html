Welcome To ICE3 2024

We are delighted to invite you to participate in ICE3 2024, the International Conference on Electrical and Electronics Engineering, which will be held in the historic and holy city of Gorakhpur, Uttar Pradesh, on November 22-23, 2024. This conference is a collaborative effort between Madan Mohan Malaviya University of Technology (MMMUT), Gorakhpur, and Atal Bihari Vajpayee-Indian Institute of Information Technology and Management (ABV-IIITM), Gwalior. The aim is to provide a platform for researchers, scientists, technocrats, academicians, and engineers to exchange innovative ideas and address new challenges in the field of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. ICE3 2024 will focus on key technological developments in areas such as wireless communication, RF microwave/radar, electrical machines, power systems, energy systems, image computing, intelligent information retrieval, intelligent agents, electrical and electronics materials, fuzzy logic control, control systems, evolutionary computing, and more. The conference is technically and financially sponsored by IEEE UP Section.

All accepted and presented papers by duly registered authors will be submitted to IEEE Xplore for publication.
